How it Works

Expected Value

EV = (Win Prob × Profit) − (Loss Prob × Stake). A positive EV means the bet is profitable over the long run.

Implied Probability

The break-even probability embedded in the odds. For -110 American odds, the implied probability is 52.4%.

Edge

Your estimated win probability minus the implied probability. A positive edge means you believe the bet is mispriced in your favor.

Kelly Criterion

The optimal fraction of your bankroll to wager, maximizing long-term growth. Use the slider to reduce variance with fractional Kelly.
